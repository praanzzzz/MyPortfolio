Features and Capabilities

HPIS includes a robust authentication system to ensure secure access for authorized users only. Healthcare providers can log in to manage patient information securely. Patient registration is simple and quick, allowing users to add new patients to the system with essential details such as name, contact information, and medical history.

The platform enables users to add detailed medical records for each patient, including:

  • **Diagnosis**: Record patient diagnoses with detailed descriptions and related information.
  • **Medications**: Log prescribed medications, including dosages, frequency, and duration.
  • **Other Medical Notes**: Add additional observations or instructions for follow-up care.

Healthcare providers can view all medical records of a patient in one place, providing a comprehensive history of diagnoses, treatments, and outcomes. This centralized access improves efficiency and supports better decision-making.

HPIS offers an organized list of all registered patients, including filters for quick searches by name, diagnosis, or other criteria. This feature is particularly useful for managing patient data at scale and for locating specific patient records quickly.

The platform includes an export functionality that allows users to download patient lists, medical records, or summaries in Excel format. This feature supports reporting, data analysis, and offline record-keeping, making it easier to share information with authorized stakeholders.
