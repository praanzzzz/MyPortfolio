Dashboard, Chat with AI, Image Analyzer

Agrosavvy is a comprehensive platform designed for agriculture, empowering users with tools for crop recommendations, data analysis, and weather alerts. The dashboard serves as a central hub for farmers and agriculture officers, offering insights into crop performance, weather forecasts, and field data management.

The Chat with AI feature leverages OpenAI's capabilities, allowing users to interact with the system to get personalized crop recommendations based on location and weather patterns. By analyzing historical data and real-time inputs, the AI helps farmers make informed decisions regarding planting, irrigation, and harvesting.

Additionally, the Image Analyzer tool uses AI to evaluate crop health by analyzing uploaded images. This feature detects pests, diseases, and other crop issues, enabling timely interventions and improving overall crop yield. With these innovative features, Agrosavvy is transforming the way agriculture professionals access information, making their processes more efficient and data-driven.

Farm Management, Automatic Coordinates, and Map Views

Agrosavvy also provides an intuitive farm management system, where users can easily add and manage farms with automatic marker placement based on geographic coordinates. When a farm is added, the platform automatically retrieves the farm's coordinates and places a marker on the map. This feature ensures accurate representation of farm locations, facilitating better management of field data and crop distribution across different areas.

The platform includes a map view, offering users the ability to visualize the location of all registered farms. This interactive map enables users to zoom in, zoom out, and click on markers to view farm details, such as farm size, crops planted, soil health, and more. The map integrates seamlessly with the database, ensuring that farm locations and their associated data are always up-to-date.

Agrosavvy also features a detailed farm list, where users can filter farms by crop type. This allows agriculture officers and farmers to quickly access relevant data based on crop selection. The list displays essential farm information, such as the farmer’s name, crop types, total area, and other key metrics. Users can easily click on each farm to view detailed information, including soil conditions, crop performance, and historical data.
